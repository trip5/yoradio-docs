commandments
  • ESP32-S3 boards vary on which GPIOs are used by HSPI bus (SCK, MISO, MOSI). Usually, no VSPI bus is available.
  • Do not use GPIOs 33 to 37 on boards with PSRAM.
  • Empty value = error.
  • GPIOs 11-17 are used by flash so if exposed are not usable.
  • GPIOs 18 and 19 are GPIs – input-only pins. They can’t be used as outputs.
  • GPIOs 20 and 21 are usually used by Serial. Some boards may use other GPIOs. Please double-check.
  • Connect display and VS RST-pin to board's RST/EN pin (value -1).
  • GPIOs 2, 8, and 9 (BOOT) are strapping pins and must be kept HIGH at boot (might be okay for push-buttons).
  • Check your Board variant here to verify SPI pins: https://github.com/espressif/arduino-esp32/tree/master/variants
