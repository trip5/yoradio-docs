commandments
  • Do not use GPIO 16-17 on WROVER module.
  • Empty value = error.
  • GPIOs 34 to 39 are GPIs – input-only pins. They can’t be used as outputs.
  • Connect display and VS RST-pin to board's RST/EN pin (value -1).
  • Try not to use GPIO 0 unnecessarily.
  • GPIOs 0 (BOOT) and 2 (LED) and 12 (MTDI) are strapping pins and must be kept HIGH at boot (might be okay for push-buttons).
  • Do not pullup GPIO 12 from the outside.
